# Temperature Sensor Connections

Black : GND Rail
Red: Vcc Rail (5V)
Yellow: GPIO4 via Breadboard. On the Breadboard, connect a resistor 
		(recommended: 10k, but works with 6.8k) between Signal pin and Vcc rail for Pull-up
